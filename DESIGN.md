# Design

### Card Types

*Unit:* A persistent character on the battlefield. Has Cost, Attack, and Health stats.
*Spell?* TODO

### Keywords

*Deploy:* An ability which activates when the card with *Deploy* is played directly from the hand.

*Echo:* An ability which activates when the card with *Echo* is destroyed.

*Guard:* Forces the opposing player to direct any unit attacks toward targets with this ability.

### Example Cards

In the format:
Name | Type:    Cost/Attack/Health - Ability

##### Playable

Frog    | Unit:      1/1/1 - *Deploy:* Deal 1 damage.
Cow     | Unit:      1/2/1 - *Deploy:* Restore 2 health.
Owl     | Unit:      2/1/1 - *Deploy:* Draw a card.
Goat    | Unit:      2/2/1 - *Echo:* Draw a card.
Bear    | Unit:      3/3/3 - *Guard*
Chicken | Unit:      3/2/3 - *Deploy:* Summon a 1/1 Chick.
